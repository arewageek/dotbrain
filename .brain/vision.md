# BIL Project Vision

## 1. Project Overview
**BIL** is a professional financial investment firm specializing in both long-term and short-term capital growth for investors in Africa and beyond. The project aims to provide a reliable and unique system that guarantees returns through investments in liquid assets and daily consumable products.

## 2. Business Model & Offerings
The business operates on a membership and investment tier system, offering significant growth opportunities for capital (8-20% profit every 15 to 30 days).

### Key Investment Products:
- **15-Day Plan**: 8% guaranteed profit.
- **30-Day Plan**: 20% guaranteed profit.
- **90-Day Fixed Plan**: 80% fixed return.
- **Eco-Investment**: A redefined investment profit and reward system centered on sustainability.
- **Referral Program**: Commission-based earnings for referring new clients.

### Bonus Structure:
- **Weekly Bonus**: $50 (Saturday - Friday).
- **C.A Bonus**: $20.
- **Quarterly Bonus**: $100.
- **RP Reward**: 20%.

## 3. Marketplace Services (Future Scope)
BIL plans to offer a diverse marketplace for its partners and investors, including:
- **Consumables**: Bakery products, Bottle Water, Drinks, Perfume.
- **Digital Services**: Recharge Cards, Data Bundles, TV Subscriptions, Bill Payments.
- **Physical Assets**: Real Estate, Architectural Services, Automobile Dealerships, Art Works (Sculpture/Paintings).
- **Fashion**: Cloth making and Fashion Design.

## 4. Target Audience
- **Primary**: Individual investors and small/large businesses in Africa seeking capital growth.
- **Secondary**: Regional partners and vendors interested in the BIL ecosystem.

## 5. Design & Aesthetic Tone
- **Professional & Trusted**: High-trust financial aesthetic utilizing **Sapphire Blue** for stability and professional reliability.
- **Growth-Oriented**: **BIL Orange** branding elements focusing on energy, success, and "the right choice" for financial future.
- **Modern & Dynamic**: Responsive UI with a focus on ease of use (email signup/verify/invest flow), using premium micro-animations and Bento-grid layouts.
- **Depth & Texture**: Use of subtle professional photography to ground the digital experience in real-world investment sectors (Consumables, Real Estate).
